ARTIST

IZ*ONE

hhhhhhhh

IZ*ONE is a south Korean-Japanese girl group formed through the Mnet reality competition show Produce 48. The group is composed of twelve members: Jang Wonyoung, Miyawaki Sakura, Jo Yu-ri, Choi Ye-na, An Yu-jin, Yabuki Nako, Kwon Eun-bi, Kang Hye-won, Honda Hitomi, Kim Chae-won, Kim Min-ju, Lee Chae-yeon.
IZ*ONE made their official debut on October 29, 2018 with their first EP Color*iz.
